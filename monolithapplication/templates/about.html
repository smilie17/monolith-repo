Our Story

Monolith Investments

Monolith Investments was registered in the Republic of Kenya on the 13th October 2015. We are a duly registered business under the Partnership Act and by YAGPO under the Youth category. Our reputation is founded on the ability to deliver quality products and efficient services to our customers.

We specialize but not limited in the supply of the following:
  1. Office Equipment
  2. Office Furniture and Tools
  3. Cleaning Materials
  4. Dry Foodstuff
  5. Kitchen,Cutlery and Staff Uniforms
  6. Hardware,plumbing,Electrical Materials and related products
  7. Tubes,Tyres and Spare Parts
  8. Stationery
  9. General Supplies
Our Mission

To provide innovative solutions backed by technology and dedicated personnel to our clients hence eliminating avoidable inefficiencies in the supply chain. We shall offer the shortest lead times as a result.

Our Vision

To be the most efficient and preferred corporate partner in its supply needs
